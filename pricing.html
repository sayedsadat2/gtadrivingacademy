Refund & Cancellation Policy

We know life happens. Here's our fair and transparent cancellation policy.

SituationPolicy
Cancel 48+ hours before lessonFull reschedule at no charge, or full credit to your account
Cancel 24–48 hours before lesson50% of lesson fee charged; remainder credited to account
Cancel less than 24 hours / No-showFull lesson fee charged (1 lesson deducted from package)
Instructor cancellation (our fault)Full reschedule at no charge + $10 goodwill credit
Package — unused lessonsUnused lessons refunded within 30 days of request
BDE program withdrawal (before 50%)Pro-rated refund for unused portion
BDE program withdrawal (after 50%)No refund — certificate cannot be issued

Cancellations must be made by phone or WhatsApp. Email cancellations are accepted but time-stamped to when we read them, not when sent.
