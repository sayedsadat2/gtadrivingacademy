Winter Driving Clinic

Ontario winters are merciless. Black ice, whiteouts, and freezing rain claim hundreds of vehicles every year. Our winter clinic gives you the hands-on skills to survive and thrive.

  • Understanding black ice and how to react
  • Skid recovery in a safe, controlled environment
  • Braking distance training on slippery surfaces
  • Whiteout visibility and low-speed maneuvers
  • Small group sessions (max 4 students)

For Teen Drivers

BDE — Beginner Driver Education

Our MTO-approved BDE program combines classroom theory with in-car training. Completion lets teen drivers take the G2 test 4 months early and may reduce insurance premiums.

  • 20 hours classroom instruction
  • 10 hours in-car training with certified instructor
  • MTO completion certificate issued
  • Eligible for insurance discount (varies by insurer)
  • G2 eligibility at 16 instead of 16+8 months
